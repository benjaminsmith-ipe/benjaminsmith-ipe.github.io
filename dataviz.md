## Tech Regulation Tracker and Timeline

The table and timeline below can be used to revisit the major legislative & regulatory milestones impacting the tech sector in the US, Europe, and China since 2017. 

### Key definitions:<br>

_**Market Structure**_ refers to regulatory actions and new legislation primarily aimed at addressing the structure, or composition, of different economic market segments. This includes new antitrust and competition legislation.<br>

_**Consumer Welfare**_ refers to regulatory actions and new legislation primarily aimed at addressing an issue that threatens the welfare of consumers. This includes data privacy, content moderation, and cybersecurity legislation.<br> 

_**Sector Specific**_ refers to regulatory actions and new legislation primarily aimed at addressing sector specific issues. This includes open banking rules and requirements for tech platforms offering financial services.
